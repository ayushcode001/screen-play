Format Guide

🎬 Scene Heading INT/EXT

Also called a "slug line". Tells us WHERE and WHEN a scene takes place. Always in UPPERCASE.

INT. DELHI MARKET — DAY
EXT. MUMBAI BEACH — NIGHT

📝 Action Line

Describe only what the camera SEES and EARS can HEAR. Write in present tense.

❌ Priya thinks about him.
✅ Priya stares at his photo, eyes filling with tears.

🎭 Character Cue

Name in ALL CAPS. Add (V.O.) for voice-over, (O.S.) if off-screen.

VIKRAM
PRIYA (V.O.)
SHARMA JI (O.S.)

💬 Dialogue

Centered block below the character name. Can mix Hindi & English (Hinglish)!

"Ek baar jo maine commitment kar li..."

( ) Parenthetical

Acting direction in brackets, placed between character name and dialogue.

(crying)
(whispering, afraid)

⟶ Transition

How we move from one scene to the next. Right-aligned, in caps.

🎵 Song Cue HINDI FILM

Unique to Hindi films! Marks where a song-and-dance sequence begins.

💡 Golden Rules

• 1 page ≈ 1 minute of screen time
• Introduce characters in CAPS first
• Hindi films average 150–170 pages
