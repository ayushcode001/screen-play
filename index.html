Format Guide

🎬 Scene Heading INT/EXT

Slug line: WHERE and WHEN. Always UPPERCASE.

INT. DELHI MARKET — DAY
EXT. MUMBAI BEACH — NIGHT

📝 Action Line

What the camera SEES and EARS can HEAR. Present tense.

✅ Priya stares at his photo, eyes filling with tears.

🎭 Character Cue

ALL CAPS. (V.O.) for voice-over, (O.S.) for off-screen.

VIKRAM
PRIYA (V.O.)

💬 Dialogue

Below character name. Mix Hindi & English freely!

"Ek baar jo maine commitment kar li..."

( ) Parenthetical

Acting direction between character and dialogue.

(crying)
(whispering, afraid)

⟶ Transition

Scene transition. Right-aligned, in caps.
